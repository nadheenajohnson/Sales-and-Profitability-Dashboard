Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales and Profitability Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze business performance across different product categories, sub-categories, and time periods. The dashboard provides key business insights through interactive visualizations and filters, enabling users to monitor sales performance and profitability effectively.

Key Features

Interactive filters for Year, State, and Category

Displays key business KPIs:

Total Sales: 1.93M

Total Profit: 247.96K

Total Quantity: 32K

Profit Margin: 12.86%


Category-wise sales and profit analysis

Sub-category profit analysis

Profit margin comparison by category

Monthly sales trend analysis

Executive summary highlighting key business insights


Key Insights

Technology generated the highest sales (704K) and profit (122K).

Office Supplies achieved the highest profit margin (17.97%).

Furniture recorded the lowest profit margin (2.87%) despite strong sales.

Tables was the largest loss-making sub-category (-12K).

Sales peaked in December (0.24M), with strong performance also observed in November and September.

Business Value
